# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 18, 2026

## Summary

learnship does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. It is a local tool that runs entirely on your machine.

## Data collection

learnship collects **no data whatsoever**. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No project content, code, or files are transmitted anywhere
- No account or registration required
- No network requests are made by learnship itself

## How learnship works

learnship installs workflow files, skills, and templates into your local AI coding tool's configuration directory (e.g. `~/.claude/`, `~/.windsurf/`). All processing happens locally between you and your AI coding tool (Claude Code, Windsurf, Cursor, etc.). learnship has no server, no backend, and no internet connectivity of its own.

## Third-party services

learnship is installed via `npx learnship` from the npm registry. The npm registry's own privacy policy applies to the installation process. Beyond installation, learnship makes no further use of any third-party services.
